Our Story

Built on Passion,
Driven by Precision

Since 2010, Imperial Motor House has set the standard for luxury automotive retail in the United Kingdom.

More Than a
Showroom

Imperial Motor House was founded on a single belief: that acquiring an exceptional vehicle should be as extraordinary as driving one. We built our showroom not as a transactional space, but as a destination — a place where passion for automotive excellence is shared.

From our Mayfair headquarters, we curate only the finest examples of modern and classic performance cars, applying rigorous standards to every vehicle that bears our name.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Excellence
Every vehicle is hand-selected and inspected against a 120-point quality standard. We accept nothing that falls short of extraordinary.
Partnership
We build long-term relationships, not single transactions. Our advisors are with you before, during, and long after purchase.
Integrity
Transparent pricing, honest provenance, and straightforward advice. You will always know exactly what you are purchasing and why.
Discretion
We understand that many of our clients value privacy above all. Confidential transactions and private viewings are available by arrangement.
Innovation
From our digital showroom to our EV charging infrastructure, we invest continuously in the future of the automotive experience.
Passion
Every member of our team is a genuine enthusiast. That passion translates into expertise, insight, and an experience that is truly different.

Our Journey

2010
Founded in Mayfair
Imperial Motor House opened its doors at 14 Mayfair Lane with an initial inventory of 12 carefully curated vehicles and a team of four.
2014
100th Vehicle Sold
We celebrated our 100th sale — a 1967 Ferrari 275 GTB/4 — marking a milestone in our reputation for sourcing exceptional cars.
2018
Showroom Expansion
We doubled our floor space, added a private viewing suite, and launched our nationwide delivery and concierge service.
2021
Digital Platform Launch
Our fully digital inventory and booking system went live, allowing clients to browse, reserve, and finance entirely online.
2026
Today
Over 2,400 vehicles sold across the UK and Europe. A team of 28 specialists. The same passion for excellence as day one.
