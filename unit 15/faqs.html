Everything you need to know about buying, financing, and owning through Imperial Motor House.

Your Questions

Contact Us Directly
How do I purchase a vehicle from Imperial Motor House?
Browse our inventory online or visit the showroom. Once you've selected a vehicle, our team will guide you through a personalised purchase process — including finance options, part-exchange valuations, and delivery arrangements. A refundable £500 reservation deposit secures the vehicle for 7 days.
Are all vehicles inspected before sale?
Every vehicle undergoes a rigorous 120-point inspection by our certified technicians. Full service history, HPI checks, and provenance documentation are included. Manufacturer warranties are honoured and extended warranty packages are available at point of purchase.
What finance options are available?
We offer Hire Purchase (HP), Personal Contract Purchase (PCP), and Personal Lease (PCH) through our panel of premium finance partners. Use our finance calculator on the Financing page for an indicative monthly payment with no impact on your credit score.
Can I part-exchange my current vehicle?
Yes. Submit your vehicle details online or bring it to the showroom for a same-day valuation. We purchase all makes and models. The part-exchange value is deducted directly from your new vehicle price or settled in cash.
How do I book a test drive?
Visit our Booking page to select your preferred vehicle, date, and time slot. You'll need a valid driving licence and proof of identity. Extended home test drives are available for select models — contact our team for details.
Is there a minimum age for a test drive?
You must be at least 21 years old and have held a full driving licence for a minimum of 12 months. For high-performance vehicles over 500 bhp, the minimum age is 25. All test drives are fully insured through our showroom policy.
Do you offer nationwide delivery?
We deliver to any mainland UK address via enclosed transporter. International delivery is available to most European destinations. White-glove home delivery — including a full handover and demonstration — is available for all vehicles.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery is typically arranged within 3–7 working days after all paperwork and finance is confirmed. Collection from our showroom can be arranged same-day in most cases. A dedicated handover specialist will prepare your vehicle and brief you on all features.
What warranty comes with my vehicle?
All vehicles come with a minimum 12-month comprehensive warranty. Manufacturer warranties are transferred where valid. Extended warranty packages of up to 3 years are available at purchase. Our aftersales team is available Monday to Saturday for support.
Do you offer servicing and maintenance?
Our approved service centre handles all makes using manufacturer-approved parts and diagnostics. Annual service plans, MOT packages, and full detailing are available. Collection and delivery for servicing can be arranged within the Greater London area.
Can I reserve a vehicle online?
Yes — a fully refundable £500 reservation fee holds any vehicle for up to 7 days while you arrange finance, test drive, or travel to the showroom. If you decide not to proceed, the deposit is returned in full within 3 business days.
Will applying for finance affect my credit score?
Our initial quote uses a soft credit search, leaving no footprint on your credit file. A formal application uses a hard search, which you authorise separately. We work with multiple lenders to find you the best rate before any hard search is conducted.
Still Have Questions?

Our team is available Monday to Saturday, 9am – 6pm.
